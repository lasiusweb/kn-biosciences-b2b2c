# Initial Concept
KN Biosciences is a comprehensive B2C/B2B e-commerce platform for agricultural and aquaculture products including bio-fertilizers and pre-probiotics.

# Product Definition

## Target Audience
- **Individual farmers and retail customers (B2C):** Users looking for sustainable agricultural and aquaculture solutions for their specific needs.
- **Business distributors and wholesale clients (B2B):** Distributors managing bulk orders and requiring streamlined quote-to-order workflows.
- **Internal staff and administrative managers:** Personnel managing the catalog, inventory, orders, and customer relationships.

## Business Goals
- **Seamless Retail Experience:** Provide an intuitive and efficient shopping experience for individual farmers.
- **Streamlined Wholesale Operations:** Simplify bulk ordering, quote requests, and approval processes for B2B distributors.
- **Operational Excellence:** Enhance inventory management with robust batch tracking and traceability for all products.

## Core Features
- **Unified E-commerce Storefront:** A dynamic, searchable product catalog optimized for both B2C and B2B segments.
- **Advanced Quote Management:** A dedicated B2B portal for requesting and approving wholesale quotes.
- **Traceable Inventory System:** Real-time stock tracking with batch-level granularity and automated low-stock alerts.
- **Integrated Admin Super-App:** A unified control center for managing the catalog, orders, inventory, CMS content, and marketing campaigns with real-time business intelligence.
