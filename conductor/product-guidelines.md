# Product Guidelines

## Brand Voice
- **Professional & Trustworthy:** We establish scientific authority and reliability as an expert in biological agricultural solutions.
- **Supportive & Accessible:** We remain approachable and community-focused, ensuring that farmers feel supported and empowered by our platform.

## Prose & Communication Style
- **Educational & Clear:** Complex biological concepts are explained in simple, accessible terms to help users understand product benefits.
- **Action-Oriented & Direct:** Content is optimized for efficiency, helping users complete tasks like ordering or checking quotes quickly.
- **Encouraging Tone:** We use a helpful and positive tone to guide users through their journey, from product selection to successful application.

## Visual Identity
- **Clean & Minimalist:** We prioritize whitespace and structural clarity to ensure a seamless experience on all devices, from mobile phones to desktop dashboards.
- **Data-Driven & Informative:** Complex information, such as batch tracking and analytics, is presented through clear, intuitive charts and tables.
- **Modern Professional Aesthetic:**
    - **Palette:** A high-contrast foundation (Light/Dark modes) featuring a vibrant **Mint Green** accent to symbolize growth, freshness, and trust.
    - **Typography:** Modern, legible sans-serif fonts (e.g., Montserrat/Outfit) for a contemporary feel.
    - **Layout:** Soft rounded corners (8px) and subtle depth through shadows to create a modern, layered interface.
